####Problem

Input: "babad"  
Output: "bab"  
Note: "aba" is also a valid answer.



#####Note

- - -
####Solution
- abbacddc  처럼 붙어있으면 저게 palindrome으로 나옴. 구분 필요  




- - - 
####Analysis
Time complexity

O(n**2)

Space complexity
O(n**2)
